Hi! Welcome to Recipe Room

My name is JA Padilla. I live in the Antipolo City in the Philippines. I created this food blog, Recipe Room, to share my best recipes to everyone. As huge fan of the Classic way of cooking, it is my goal to teach people how to cook classic dishes using the simplest way possible. Aside for my deep passion in cooking and my continuous craving for the distinctive Classic food taste, I was motivated to create cooking videos in order to promote classic food. I noticed that Classic Food is not well represented inside of the Philippines. It is time for classic food to be noticed, known, tasted, and enjoyed by the rest of the Filipinos.
